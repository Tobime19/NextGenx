Corporate Overview

NextGen Engineering Works (formerly System Engineering Works, established in 2003) is a premier industrial engineering and manufacturing enterprise based in New Delhi. We specialize in the high-tolerance fabrication of plastic moulds, interlocking concrete paver moulds, heavy consolidation vibrator tables, color mixers, and custom machinery.

We cater to bulk B2B procurement needs across India and Nepal, providing state-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities backed by rigorous ISO 9001 compliance standards. Our plant utilizes advanced multi-axis CNC machines to translate complex CAD designs into high-durability physical tools.
